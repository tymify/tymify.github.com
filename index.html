Collect your Stamps

Tymify uses Stamps. What is a Stamp? Stamps can be anything public, and date related. What makes Stamps awesome is that they can be the container for a yet to be defined moment. Set an empty Stamp for your new event, and fill it with Time-Stamps as your planning unfolds. A Stamp can contain multiple Time-Stamps, and Time-Stamps can be added to a Stamp whenever you like. This allows you to track or create Stamps for anything; from opening ticket sales to an event, episode air dates for your favorite TV series, to the release of a new software feature.

Target and reach more people

Stamps represent a better way to follow an upcoming moment in time. Multiple Time-Stamps within your Stamp mean your target audiences never miss an important related moment. As soon as someone tracks your Stamp, they will be updated with the upcoming related Time-Stamps as and when you define them.

Sync & start to import Stamps

Create your Stamps in one click from the import feed. You can use iCal Feed, Google Calendar, Facebook, Eventbrite .... We handle the import and you decide which Stamp you want to create.
